Backdate your State Pension

You'll get a one-off payment for any State Pension we owe you from the date you enter to today (14 July 2022).

The earliest date you can backdate to is 15 July 2021.

Any State Pension we owe you from before the date you enter will be used to increase your payments.

They increase by just over 0.1% for every week between 5 March 2021 and the date you enter. It takes over 17 years to get back the money you use to increase your payments.

The smallest increase you can get is 1%, so you'll need to enter a date of 8 August 2021 or later.
