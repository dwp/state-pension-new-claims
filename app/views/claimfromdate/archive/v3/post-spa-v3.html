Your State Pension date

You reached State Pension age on 28 April 2021 and may be owed some State Pension.

You can use any State Pension we owe you to increase your payments for the rest of your life.

They increase by just over 0.1% for every week you delay your start date, as long as you enter a date after 30 June 2021.

Find out more about delaying your State Pension (opens in a new tab).

You can calculate your State Pension from any date between 25 June 2021 and 25 Otober 2022.

Your State Pension payments will not start until after the date you enter.

Any State Pension you do not use to increase your payments will be paid along with first payment.
