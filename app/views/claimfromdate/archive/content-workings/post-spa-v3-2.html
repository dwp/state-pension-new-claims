What date do you want to start your State Pension from?

A date in the past

To get some of the State Pension we owe you as a one-off payment and use the rest to increase your payments for the rest of your life, enter a date between 14 August 2021 and 14 June 2022.

The earlier your start date is, the bigger your one-off arrears payment will be.

The later your start date is, the more your payments will increase.

A date in the future

To increase your State Pension payments further enter a date between 14 June 2022 and 14 October 2022. You will not receive any State Pension payments until the date you enter.
For example, 27 3 2007
