Your State Pension date

You reached State Pension age on 11 April 2021 and may be owed some State Pension. You can:

  • backdate your State Pension and get a one-off arrears payment
  • choose a start date after 14 June 2021 and increase your payments for the rest of your life

One off arrears payment

To get up to 12 months of the State Pension we owe you as a one-off payment, enter a date before today 14 June 2022.

The earlier your start date is, the bigger your one-off arrears payment will be.

Increase your payments

To increase your State Pension payments enter a date after 14 June 2021. The later your start date, the bigger your increase will be.

You will not receive any State Pension payments until the date you enter.

What date do you want your State Pension to start from?
