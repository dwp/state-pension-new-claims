Iteration 3

payment services research
Date of design
October 2019
Designers
Emily Wilkinson and Colin Jones

Scenario

  • A payment had been returned by the bank because the citizen has changed their bank details. Agents need to mark the payment as not paid
  • Citizen then rings up and provides the agent with new bank details, agent enters the new bank details and re-issues any returned payments

Business requirements

  • Timeline may fail, there is a need to let agents know that their action has been completed. Tested alert banner pattern

Iterations based on previous research

  • Moved the from and to dates into details link to make payment history table more accessible
  • Agents can only change a status based on the current status e.g. paid can only change to returned

Research notes

  • Agents identified the 're-issue payment' alert after they marked the payment as returned. They then tried to re-issue the payment before changing the bank details
  • Agents said the terminology used for the payment status was not what they would use. For example they would use 'returned' rather than 'not paid'
