Date of design

Designers

Scenarios

  • Check the record of the claimant's spouse to see if there is any change to their State Pension entitlement.

Research notes

  • Agents understood what the task was before they continued into action it
  • All agents believed the information displayed for the claimant and their spouse was needed for this task
  • Some agents thought having the claimant National Insurance number would also be useful for tracing the spouse
  • All agents able to easily navigate through and complete the task

Business requirements

  • Tasks will be ordered by the claimants SPA date and the date the claim was received
