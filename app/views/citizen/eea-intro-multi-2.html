Claiming European Union (EU) pensions.

You may be entitled to EU pensions from France, Spain and Germany based on the time you lived or worked there.

If you want to make a claim for your EU pensions, we will send you some forms to complete.

If you have already claimed your EU pension from a country, you do not need to claim again.

EU pensions do not affect how much UK State Pension you will get.
