Applying for a pension from Germany

You may be entitled to a European Union (EU) pension from Germany based on the time you lived or worked there.

If you want to apply for your pension from Germany, we will send you a form by post to complete and return to us.Â 

If you have already applied for your pension from Germany, you do not need to apply again.

EU pensions do not affect how much UK State Pension you will get.
