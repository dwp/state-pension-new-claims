Applying for European Union (EU) pensions

You may be entitled to EU pensions from Belgium and the France based on the time you lived or worked there.

If you want to apply for your EU pensions, we will send you a form by post to complete and return to us.

If you have already applied for your EU pension from a country, you do not need to apply again.

EU pensions do not affect how much UK State Pension you will get.
