Claiming a pension from France.

You may be entitled to a European Union (EU) pension based on the time you lived or worked in France.

If you want to make a claim for your pension from France, we will send some forms to complete.

If you have already claimed your pension from France, you do not need to claim again.

EU pensions do not affect how much UK State Pension you will get.
