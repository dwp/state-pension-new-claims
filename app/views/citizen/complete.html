If your circumstances change

You must tell us straight away if:

  • you change address or move into a care home
  • you move or intend to move to another country permanently
  • you change bank details
  • you go to prison or are held in legal custody
  • you start or stop getting another social security benefit
  • you get married or form a civil partnership
  • you get divorced or your civil partnership is dissolved
  • your husband, wife or civil partner dies

Telephone: 0800 731 0469
Textphone: 0800 731 0464

Report changes as soon as they happen because they may affect how much State Pension you get. If you do not tell us about any changes, you may be paid too much and have to pay this back.
