Your State Pension date

You reached State Pension age on 28 April 2021 and may be owed some State Pension. You can split any money we owe you between:

  • an arrears payment
  • extra State Pension for the rest of your life

One off arrears payment

You could get up to 12 months of the State Pension we owe you as a one-off arrears payment.

The earlier the date you enter, the bigger your one-off arrears payment will be.

The earliest date you can enter is 25 June 2021, this date would give you the biggest arrears payment you can get.

Extra State Pension

You can also use any State Pension we owe you to increase your payments for the rest of your life.

The later the date you enter, the bigger your increase will be. You must enter a date after 30 June 2021 and before 25 October 2022.

If you enter a date in the future your State Pension payments will start after that date.

Find out more about delaying your State Pension (opens in a new tab).
