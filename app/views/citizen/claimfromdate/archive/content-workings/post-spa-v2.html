What date do you want to start your State Pension?

You reached State Pension age on 11 April 2021 and may be owed some State Pension.

Enter a start date:

  • of 11 April 2021 to get all of the State Pension you are owed as a one-off arrears payment
  • after 14 June 2021 to increase your State Pension payments for the rest of your life - the later your start date, the bigger your increase will be
  • before 20 June 2022 to get a one-off arrears payment - the earlier your start date, the bigger your one-off payment will be
