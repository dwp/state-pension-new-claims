Delaying your start date

You can backdate your pension to 12 April 2022 or delay getting it until a later date.

Find out more about delaying your State Pension (opens in a new tab).

Do you want to delay your start date?
Your State Pension increases by just over 0.1% for every week that you delay, as long as you delay for at least 9 weeks.
You will get any State Pension we owe you as a one-off arrears payment along with your first payment.
