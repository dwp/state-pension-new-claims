Your State Pension date

You reached State Pension age on 5 May 2021 and may be owed some State Pension. You can split any money we owe you between:

  • extra State Pension for the rest of your life
  • a one-off arrears payment


Extra State Pension

Your State Pension increases by just over 0.1% a week between 5 May 2021 and the start date you choose, as long as you choose a date after 14 June 2021.

The later your start date, the bigger your increase will be. You must enter a start date between 6 July 2021 and 31 October 2022

If you enter a date in the future your payments will start after that date.

You will get the extra State Pension for the rest of your life but it can take more than 17 years to get back the money that you deferred.



Backdate your State Pension

Any State Pension you are owed from the last 12 months that you do not use to increase your payments will be paid as a one-off arrears payment.

The earlier your start date, the bigger your one-off arrears payment will be.
