Overview

Specialist agent team (Section 10) will check if there a claim who’s citizen was a former employee of Maxwell.

MQP not met with FRY posting
Date added Change log Link
31 October 2024

Findings

  • Link to the design ideation Mural board
  • Link to project goals and theory in Confluence

Overview

Final relevant year might be the last tax year in which the citizen made contributions or had earnings before they apply for state pension.

For this initiative, the following must be considered:

  • postpone a task when the citizen’s record show 9+ years NI contribution and the final relevant year is not yet posted
  • there is a possibility that the citizen might achieve MQP once the FRY is posted
  • build a functionality in place where we can postpone the task for 6 months
  • if the citizen is applying before 6th April and they have 9 years → Wait for FRY to be posted for last year → Postpone the task for 6 months until the FRY is posted

Mixed overlapping benefits


Allow citizens with mixed overlapping benefits to be processed on GySP

Date added Change log Link
7 October 2024

Findings

  • Link to the design ideation Mural board

Overview

Task heirarchy has been established and a prototype was built to illustrate the 4 ways that mixed overlapping benefits can be combined and resolved.

The order of the tasks is as follows: first - War Pensions, then - Carer's Allowance or Carer's Support Payment, and finally - Pension Credit.

  • Scenario 1 - War Pensions, then Carer's Allowance (or Carer Support Payment), then Pension Credit
  • Scenario 2 - War Pensions, then Carer's Allowance (or Carer Support Payment)
  • Scenario 3 - War Pensions, then Pension Credit
  • Scenario 4 - Carer's Allowance (or Carer Support Payment), then Pension Credit
