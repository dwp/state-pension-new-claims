Confirm disallowance

Check the claimant's details in NIRs to confirm they are not entitled to any State Pension. You need to check the claimant:

  • has less than 10 qualifying years
  • has no RRE on their record
  • is not entitled to any award based on a late spouse or partner
  • has no pension share order on divorce
  • has not lived or worked abroad in an EEA country or a country with a reciprocal agreement
