This page is only to explain operational procedure and will not be part of the journey.

After the options letter has been sent to the claimant:

  1. The task is postponed for 2 months to allow a response from the claimant.
  2. If the claimant has not called with their decision within 2 months, a reminder letter is automatically sent to the claimant, telling them what will happen if they do not respond to the letter. The task is postponed for another month.
  3. If we still have not heard back from the claimant a month after the reminder was sent, the lump sum inheritance is automatically applied to the claimant's account at a 20% tax rate.
