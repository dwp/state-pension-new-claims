This page is only to explain operational procedure and will not be part of the journey.

After the options letter has been sent to the claimant:

  1. The task is postponed for 3 weeks to allow a response, either by post or by phone, from the claimant.
  2. If we haven't gotten a response within 3 weeks, a reminder letter is automatically sent to the claimant, telling them what will happen if they do not respond to the letter. The task is postponed for another week.
  3. If we still have not heard back from the claimant after the second postponment period, the lump sum inheritance is automatically applied to the claimant's account.
