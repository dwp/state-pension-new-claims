Entitlement to European Union (EU) pensions

Explain to the claimant that based on their answers, they may be entitled to EU pensions from {% if data['country-5'] %} {{data['country-1']}}, {{data['country-2']}}, {{data['country-3']}}, {{data['country-4']}} and {{data['country-5']}}. {% elseif data['country-4'] %} {{data['country-1']}}, {{data['country-2']}}, {{data['country-3']}}, and {{data['country-4']}}. {% elseif data['country-3'] %} {{data['country-1']}}, {{data['country-2']}}, and {{data['country-3']}}. {% else %} {{data['country-1']}} and {{data['country-2']}}. {% endif %}

If the claimant wants to apply for their EU pensions, we’ll send a form out by post. Explain to them that when we get the completed form, we’ll send a claim to the relevant Foreign Authorities. The Foreign Authorities will review the claim and confirm whether or not the claimant can get a pension from them.

You may need to tell them that EU pensions do not affect how much State Pension the claimant will get.
